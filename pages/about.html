About LawFIRM

India's Premier Full-Service Law Firm

Established in 2003, LawFIRM has grown to become one of India's most respected and trusted legal service providers. With our headquarters in New Delhi and offices across Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, and Kolkata, we serve clients throughout India and internationally.

Our firm is built on the foundation of excellence, integrity, and client-focused service. We combine deep expertise in Indian law with innovative legal strategies to deliver exceptional results for our clients. Whether you're an individual, a startup, or a multinational corporation, we provide personalized legal solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Our team of 50+ highly qualified attorneys brings together diverse expertise spanning corporate law, litigation, family law, criminal defense, intellectual property, taxation, and more. We are committed to defending your rights and protecting your future with unwavering dedication.

Our Vision

To be recognized as India's most trusted and innovative law firm, setting new standards of excellence in legal practice. We envision a future where access to justice is a reality for everyone, supported by cutting-edge legal solutions.

Our Values

Our Story

Two Decades of Legal Excellence in India

2003 - The Beginning

LawFIRM was founded in New Delhi by Senior Advocate Rajesh Kumar with a vision to provide high-quality legal services to individuals and businesses across India. Started with a team of 5 dedicated attorneys.

2003

2008 - Expansion

Opened offices in Mumbai and Bangalore. Expanded practice areas to include corporate law, intellectual property, and international arbitration. Team grew to 20 attorneys.

2008

2015 - Recognition

Won landmark cases in Supreme Court and High Courts. Recognized as one of India's Top 10 Law Firms by Legal 500. Established offices in Chennai and Kolkata.

2015

2020 - Innovation

Launched digital legal services platform. Implemented AI-powered legal research tools. Expanded team to 50+ attorneys specializing in various fields of Indian law.

2020

2024 - Present Day

Completed 5000+ successful cases. Serving clients across India and internationally. Continued commitment to excellence with 98% success rate and thousands of satisfied clients.
