About Advocate Rajesh Kumar

Advocate Rajesh Kumar is the Founder and Senior Partner at LawFIRM, bringing over 25 years of distinguished legal practice in Corporate Law, Mergers & Acquisitions, and Securities Law. He is recognized as one of India's leading corporate law experts and has been instrumental in shaping some of the country's landmark corporate transactions.

A graduate of National Law School of India University (NLSIU) Bangalore and holder of an LL.M. from Harvard Law School, USA, Adv. Kumar has represented Fortune 500 companies, leading Indian corporations, and government entities in complex corporate matters. His expertise spans across company law, corporate restructuring, private equity, venture capital, and cross-border M&A.

Adv. Kumar has been recognized by international legal directories including Chambers & Partners, Legal 500, and IFLR1000 as a leading lawyer in Corporate/M&A. He has also been a visiting faculty at several premier law schools in India and regularly speaks at national and international legal conferences.
